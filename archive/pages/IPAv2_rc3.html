Overview of FreeIPA v2 Release Candidate 2¶

The FreeIPA project team is pleased to announce the availability of the Release Candidate 3 release of freeIPA 2.0 server. If no critical problems are found this will become the final release.

  • Binaries are available for F-14 and F-15.

  • Please do not hesitate to share feedback, criticism or bugs with us on our mailing list: mailto:freeipa-users@redhat.com

Main Highlights of Release Candidate 3¶

This release candidate has a set of significant improvements across all areas of the project. Modifications include but are not limited to:

  • i18n improvements

  • Fixed the self-service page in the WebUI

  • Use TLS for CA replication

  • Setting up Winsync agreements has been fixed

Known Issues¶

  • Installing IPA on Fedora-15 works but can take more time than Fedora 14 due to systemd. It is not recognizing some restarts as being successful so only continues after a 3-minute timeout. We are working on a solution.
